# 01E_RISK_AND_COMPLIANCE

## Purpose

Keep the project legally, ethically and operationally safe.

## Allowed focus

- Offline replay analysis.
- Self-play simulator.
- Model training and evaluation.
- Risk and strategy analysis.
- Human-reviewed decision support.
- Compliance-reviewed deployment paths.

## Not allowed without explicit compliance clearance

- Unauthorized platform automation.
- Account evasion or anti-detection.
- Captcha, rate limit, access-control or anti-cheat bypass.
- Secret storage in repo.
- Unauthorized data scraping or redistribution.

## Risk table

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Unsupported strength claims | High | Require documented evaluation | Research lead | Open |
| Tenhou automation compliance | High | Use offline/self-play first; document platform rules before any integration | Project owner | Open |
| Data rights ambiguity | Medium | Track data source and license | Data owner | Open |
